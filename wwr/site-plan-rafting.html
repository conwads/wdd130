Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to give informative information about white water rafting. Also, we lay out the price, location, and accomodations of the actual area. We hope that this website will not only give a clear idea of what your experience will be like but, that it will also make your experience with us more enjoyable!

Audience

Our target audience is anyone between the ages of 18 - 40 since you must be at least 18 years of age to sign the waivor and be accountable. We highly discourage individuals above the age of 40 due to the high risk of cardiac issues because of the high adrenaline rush you will experience.
